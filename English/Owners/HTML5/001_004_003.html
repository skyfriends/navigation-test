4.3 Extruder Startup Initial Conditions Checklist

The following should be verified prior to attempting any type of the extruder system startup:

  1. That the extruder system control panel is powered up.
  2. That the PLC is powered up and running normally (as per the program status displayed on the front face of the PLC module).
  3. That each E-Stop pushbutton is in the popped-up position and that the E-Stop Reset button has been pressed.
  4. That the system steam, water, and air pressures are all within normal ranges (use the Overview Screen).
  5. That the live bin, feeder, and preconditioner doors are closed and locked, and if Fortress locks are used that the keys are all inserted into the proofing housing and locked in place.
  6. That the downspout is closed and locked and in the Reject position.
  7. That the knifehood is closed and locked.
  8. That the knife disconnect switch on the knife stand is in the On position.
  9. That the dry feed test mode is NOT the selected operation scheme (the extruder status in the top left corner of the Automated Process Management (APM) monitor should be displayed as extruder stopped).
  10. That the desired product formula has been selected and loaded into the PLC.
  11. That the desired dry feed rate control loop setpoint has been specified.
  12. That all device control loops on the Overview Screen are set to Auto control mode (i.e., background color for each device's status/control ellipse should be white; none should be light blue or yellow). NOTE: that the extruder zone temperature control loops are set by the PLC software to Auto control mode upon startup of the extruder or upon initiation of a prestart. Then, when the extruder is shut down the temperature control loops will be set to Manual control mode.
  13. That all pump, agitator and refill control loops on the Liquid Screen(s) are set to Auto control mode.
  14. That there are no active (unacknowledged, red text) alarms.
