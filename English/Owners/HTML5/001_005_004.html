5  Alarms

5.4 Quick Shutdown Alarms

When any one of the alarms occurs that are listed in Table 5.2, the PLC executes a quick extruder system shutdown sequence (the downspout diverts, all devices upstream of it are disabled, and the normal shutdown sequence is then executed.). Each alarm that is listed below is assigned a number for reference only (it does not reflect any priority, severity, or importance). For each quick shutdown alarm you will find listed below its unique APM alarm reference number and alarm text, both appearing in the alarm region of the screen header and on the Alarm Summary screen. You will also find the most probable cause and some troubleshooting suggestions for each alarm on Table 5.2.

Table 5.2 Quick Extruder System Shutdown
Alarm Number Alarm Text Most Likely Cause Possible Solutions
16 Shutdown:
Low System Air Pressure 		A shutdown sequence has been initiated because of a low system air pressure condition. See alarm number 37
17 Shutdown:
Knife Motor Off 		A shutdown sequence has been initiated because of a knife motor is Off condition. TBD
18 Shutdown:
High Extruder Motor Load 		A shutdown sequence has been initiated because of a high extruder motor load condition. See alarm number 149
19 Shutdown:
Low/Low Extruder Steam Flow 		A shutdown sequence has been initiated because of a low/low extruder steam flow condition. See alarm number 107
20 Shutdown:
Low/Low Extruder Steam Flow 		A shutdown sequence has been initiated because of a low/low extruder water flow condition. See alarm number 111
21 Shutdown:
High Conehead Pressure 		A shutdown sequence has been initiated because of a high conehead pressure condition. See alarm number 153
22 Shutdown:
Cylinder Motor Fault 		A shutdown has occurred because of a preconditioner motor fault condition. See alarm number 124, 125, 126 or 127
23 Shutdown:
Downstream Not Ready 		A shutdown has occurred because processing equipment downstream of the extruder system is not ready to accept raw product. See alarm number 173
28 Shutdown:
Knife Motor Fault 		A shutdown has occurred because of a knife motor fault condition. See alarm number 156
